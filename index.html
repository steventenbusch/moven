Dearest Morgan,

What a year it has been.

We got married. We spent weeks exploring Greece together. You moved into our apartment. You quit your job. We moved halfway across the country. You started a new job. You started a new business. We almost bought a house.

When I list it all out, it sounds like a whirlwind. But somehow it doesn't feel like we're moving too fast. It feels like we're building a life together—one adventure, one decision, and one dream at a time.

We have so much to look back on and be proud of, and even more to look forward to. A family. A thriving business. A renovated home. A lake house someday. Rental properties. More travel. More hiking. More swimming. More laughter. More peace. More time spent simply being in each other's presence.

I am so grateful to be on this journey with you. No matter what comes next, there is no one else I would rather build a life with.

I love you so, so much.

I created this website as a place to preserve our story—a home for our memories, our adventures, fun little games, and anything else I dream up that I want to share with you. My hope is that over the years it becomes a collection of the life we build together and a reminder of just how much I love you.

Happy Anniversary.

Love always,
